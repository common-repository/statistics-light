Statistics-Light - A lightweight statistic tool for Wordpress.
 
== Description ==
 
Statistics-Light - A lightweight statistic tool for Wordpress.


Collected data:
Visited website (URL, WP PostID), Referrer, time/time of access and the IP address of the user. 
The IP address is automatically deleted after 24 hours. 

The data remains solely with you and cannot be viewed and evaluated by third parties.

The app makes it possible not only to visualize the data such as visitor numbers clearly for your blog. 
You also have the possibility to add several pages in the app, if you run several blogs.

App for iOS and Android - Features:
+ Management of 1 to 36 Wordpress blogs.
+ Simple titles and color management
+ Blog statistics are protected by individual paths and passwords.
+ 3D-View of visitor and access statistics after 7, 14 and 30 days.

== Installation ==
  
1. Upload `statistics-light` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Optional: Activate Data-API to visualize the statistics data on your mobile device (statistics-light App).
